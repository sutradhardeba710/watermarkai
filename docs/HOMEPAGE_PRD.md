# Homepage PRD

## Purpose

Replace the Phase 1 landing page with a production-ready homepage that explains the authorized video-cleanup workflow, builds trust, and directs qualified visitors to registration or login.

## Goals

- Convert new visitors to /register and returning users to /login.
- Explain upload, mask, preview, and export without overpromising.
- Keep ownership and authorization requirements prominent.
- Deliver a responsive, accessible marketing experience.

## Scope

The homepage includes a branded header, hero, product illustration, workflow, capability cards, authorized-use callout, and footer. It does not upload files, make pricing claims, or present unsupported testimonials or metrics.

## Acceptance criteria

- Every CTA uses an existing route.
- Semantic headings, landmarks, and keyboard focus states are present.
- The layout has no horizontal overflow at 320 px and scales to desktop.
- Content matches existing ownership-confirmation controls.
- Type checking and the production build pass.
